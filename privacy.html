Privacy of the Living

Last Updated: January 2026 — Your secrets are safe with us (until the end)

1. Introduction

Welcome to Are you dead?! We care about your privacy as much as you care about staying alive. This policy explains how we handle your data while you're still walking among us.

By using Are you dead?, you agree that we can store just enough info to make sure someone knows if you've stopped breathing.

2. Our Data Collection Principle

Minimal Data for Maximum Safety:

We collect only the essential information needed to provide our safety services. Your privacy is paramount, and we use this data solely to help your "Retrieval Squad" and "Chosen One" keep track of your status.

  • We use your Device ID to identify you—this allows the app to function without complex accounts.
  • We DO NOT sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.
  • We DO NOT track your real-time GPS location.
  • We only store what is active—older check-in data is overwritten by the new.

3. What We Collect & Why

To implement friend sharing and emergency notification features, we collect the following information:

  • Device ID: Used as your unique identifier to manage your status and connections without requiring personal account information.
  • Check-in Data: We store your latest check-in time and latest check-in photo. These are used to show your friends you're safe. We only keep the most recent one—older records are overwritten.
  • Emergency Contact Details: The name and email of your Chosen One, so we know who to mail when you go quiet.
  • Safety Settings: Your timeout duration and the custom message you want us to send to your emergency contact.

This data is used exclusively for friend information sharing and automated emergency emails.

4. How the Magic Happens Without Your Data

You might wonder: "How does this work if you don't actively collect data?"

  • Local-First Design: Most of your settings and history live strictly on your device.
  • Real-Time Sync: Our server acts as a temporary bridge to tell your Retrieval Squad that you're still "warm". Once the status is updated for your friends, the server has done its job.
  • Emergency Alerts: If you miss a check-in, an automated system triggers an alert to your emergency contact. No human looks at this data.

5. Data Storage (Spoiler: There Isn't Much)

5.1 Local Storage

Most of your data stays on your device. We don't have servers full of your life history because we don't actively collect it in the first place.

5.2 Data Retention & Auto-Deletion

Since we don't actively collect your data beyond what's needed for the check-in sync, there's nothing much to retain. However, to keep our "World of the Living" fresh, if you fail to check in for 20 consecutive days, your account and all associated local sync data will be automatically deleted. It's our way of saying "RIP" to your digital presence if you've truly gone AFK.

6. Your Rights (You Have All of Them)

Since we don't actively collect your data, you have complete control:

  • Full Control: All your data is on your device. Delete the app, and it's gone.
  • No Tracking: We can't track you because we don't actively collect anything to track with.
  • Privacy by Design: Your privacy isn't a feature—it's the foundation of how we built this app.
