Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Died Yet?! We understand the importance of privacy to you, and we have developed this policy to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

This policy applies to the Died Yet? application and its related services. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Information

To provide a better experience, we may collect the following device information:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Application version
  • Unique device identifier (used to generate an anonymous User ID)
  • Screen resolution and display settings

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect usage data to improve our product:

  • Frequency and duration of app usage
  • Feature usage statistics (e.g., how often you check in)
  • Error logs and crash reports

2.3 Emergency Contact Information

Necessary Information: To send alerts when you miss a check-in, we need the name and email address of your emergency contact. This information is used solely for sending safety alert emails.

2.4 Check-in Records

We record the timestamps of your check-ins to calculate streaks and trigger alerts if you miss a check-in. These records are stored on our secure servers.

3. Information We DO NOT Collect

Our Privacy Promise:
  • We DO NOT collect your real-time location.
  • We DO NOT access your contacts (unless you manually enter contact info).
  • We DO NOT perform any ad tracking.
  • We DO NOT sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Purpose of Information Use

We use collected information for:

  • Service Provision: Maintaining core check-in and notification functionality.
  • Safety Protection: Notifying your emergency contacts when missed check-ins are detected.
  • Product Improvement: Analyzing anonymous usage data to optimize user experience.

5. Information Storage and Protection

5.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers:

  • Using industry-standard encryption.
  • Data transmission via HTTPS protocol.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

5.2 Data Retention

We only retain your data for the necessary period:

  • Local data: Until you delete the application.
  • Account data: Until you delete your account.
  • Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for up to 2 years.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: View the personal information we collect.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (account deletion).
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.
