"Still alive? Check in." - Guarding your every day

An invisible safety net designed for those living alone. Check in once a day to confirm you're safe. If you miss a check-in, the system automatically notifies your emergency contacts. Never let care be absent, keeping safety online at all times.

Core Features

Simple design, solid protection

One-Tap Check-in

Just one tap a day to let the system and your loved ones know you're safe. Minimal interaction that won't disrupt your daily life.

Emergency Notification

Set up your emergency contacts. If you haven't checked in within your set timeframe (e.g., 3 days), an automated SOS will be sent via email.

Guardian Circle

Invite friends or family to join your circle. Watch over each other's status and reach out immediately if someone stops updating.

Friendly Nudge

Notice a friend in your circle forgot to check in? Send a friendly "Nudge" notification to show you care without adding pressure.

Privacy Focused

We don't track your real-time location or read your contacts. All data is encrypted and only used for your safety, respecting your privacy.

Cross-Platform Sync

Available on iOS, Android, and macOS. Your check-in history and settings stay synced across all your devices for continuous protection.
